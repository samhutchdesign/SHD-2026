# Services & Productized Offers

> Packaged offers the quiz routes to. Each states who it's for, what you get, timeline, and a starting price range. Prices are **placeholders for Sam to confirm** — based on senior market signals (~$85–$150+/hr, strategy-capable at the top). All are anchors, not final quotes.

## Why productize

Buyers convert better on clear, fixed-scope offers with a starting price than on open-ended "let's talk." Packages signal seniority and make the book-a-call CTA lower-risk. Keep 3 core offers (one per quiz track) plus an optional retainer.

---

## Offer 1 — Brand Website *(Track A: small business / brand)*

- **Who it's for:** Founders, small businesses, and creators who need a credible, conversion-minded website without becoming a web designer.
- **What you get:** Strategy mini-session → site map & copy direction → custom design → built, responsive, fast site → launch support. Mobile-first, SEO basics, analytics.
- **Timeline:** ~3–5 weeks.
- **Starting price:** *$X,XXX (fixed).* — confirm.
- **Positioning note:** Productized and efficient. This is the "I just need a website" landing spot — welcomed, but not the headline.

## Offer 2 — Product Design Sprint / Engagement *(Track B: startup / product)*

- **Who it's for:** Startups and teams building or improving an app/platform who need senior product design and UX.
- **What you get:** Discovery & strategy → user flows and journey mapping → UX/UI design → prototype → design system foundations → dev-ready handoff (and collaboration with your engineers).
- **Timeline:** Sprint (~2–3 weeks) or ongoing monthly engagement.
- **Starting price:** *Sprint from $X,XXX; monthly from $X,XXX.* — confirm.
- **Positioning note:** The core premium offer. Emphasize simplifying complexity, design systems, and working with their team.

## Offer 3 — Product Strategy *(Track B / "not sure yet")*

- **Who it's for:** Founders who need direction *before* committing to a build — what to build, for whom, and why.
- **What you get:** Goal & user definition → opportunity and flow mapping → prioritized product direction → a clear, actionable plan (and the design starting point). Reframes a tangled idea into an obvious path.
- **Timeline:** ~1–2 weeks, or a focused workshop.
- **Starting price:** *Workshop from $X,XXX; engagement from $X,XXX.* — confirm.
- **Positioning note:** The entry point for "not sure yet" quiz answers. Sells Sam's #1 strength (strategy) and naturally leads to Offer 2.

## Optional — Design Partner Retainer

- **Who it's for:** Teams who want ongoing senior design without a full-time hire.
- **What you get:** A set block of design/strategy capacity each month; priority availability.
- **Pricing:** Monthly retainer, *from $X,XXX/mo.* — confirm.

## Engagement model notes (also feeds [[faq-objections]])

- **Design-led, build-capable:** Sam designs and can build the website (Track A); for products (Track B), delivers dev-ready design and collaborates with the client's engineers. Be explicit on each offer about whether code is included.
- **Pricing approach:** fixed-scope packages with starting prices; custom quote after the discovery call. Avoid pure hourly framing publicly.
- **Every offer → book a discovery call** as the next step (matches quiz CTA).
