# Services & Productized Offers

> Packaged offers the quiz routes to. Each states who it's for, what you get, timeline, and a starting price range. Prices are **placeholders for Sam to confirm** — based on senior market signals (~$85–$150+/hr, strategy-capable at the top). All are anchors, not final quotes.

## Why productize

Buyers convert better on clear, fixed-scope offers with a starting price than on open-ended "let's talk." Packages signal seniority and make the book-a-call CTA lower-risk. Keep 3 core offers (one per quiz track) plus an optional retainer.

## Offer 1 — Marketing Website *(Track A: small business / brand)* — PACKAGED

- **Who it's for:** Founders, small businesses, and creators who need a credible, conversion-minded marketing website without becoming a web designer.
- **What you get:** Strategy mini-session → site map & copy direction → custom design (Figma) → **built and launched in Framer** → responsive, fast, mobile-first, SEO basics, analytics, launch support.
- **Build reality (important):** Sam designs in Figma and **builds simple marketing sites himself in Framer**. This is a true end-to-end package. (E-commerce: possible but not yet proven — scope case by case.)
- **Timeline:** ~3–5 weeks.
- **Pricing tiers — region-based PLACEHOLDERS for Sam to confirm.** Sam can charge more for US clients than EU. Show US visitors USD pricing and EU visitors EUR pricing (see implementation note). Client pays the Framer subscription (~$10–$30/mo) separately.

  | Tier | Scope | EU (EUR) | US (USD) |
  |---|---|---|---|
  | **Launch** | 1–3 pages / landing | €800–€1,500 | $1,500–$2,500 |
  | **Standard** | 5–7 page marketing site (core) | €2,500–€4,500 | $3,500–$5,000 |
  | **Signature** | 8+ pages, CMS/blog, bespoke motion | €5,000–€8,000 | $6,000–$8,000+ |

  Plus **custom add-ons** beyond a tier (extra pages, copywriting, integrations).
  *Research basis: 2026 US senior freelance marketing sites ~$3k–$6k (5–10 pp), $1.5k–$8k overall; EU/NL SMB sites €500–€3k typical, NL freelancers €3k–€9k for fuller scope, Western-EU senior rates €75–€150/hr.*

- **Region-based pricing — implementation note:** geolocate the visitor (IP or timezone) and display US vs EU tier + currency automatically; fall back to a region toggle (US / Europe) and a "prices in your region — confirmed on our call" line. Keep it honest: the quote is finalized after the discovery call.
- **Positioning note:** The productized offer. This is the "I just need a website" landing spot — welcomed, but not the headline.

## Offer 2 — Product Design Sprint / Engagement *(Track B: startup / product)* — BESPOKE

- **Who it's for:** Startups and teams building or improving an app/platform who need senior product design, UX, and strategy.
- **What you get:** Discovery & strategy → user flows and journey mapping → UX/UI design (Figma) → **interactive prototype** (Figma, or coded prototypes via Claude) → design-system foundations → **dev-ready handoff to your engineers.**
- **Build reality (important):** Sam designs and prototypes; he is **not a developer** and does not build/ship production apps or complex sites. Your team (or one Sam helps you find) builds it. Be explicit about this — it's a strength (senior design focus), not a gap to hide.
- **Timeline:** Sprint (~2–3 weeks) or ongoing monthly engagement.
- **Pricing:** **Bespoke** — scoped to product size and needs, quoted after a discovery call. No fixed package.
- **Positioning note:** The core premium offer. Emphasize simplifying complexity, design systems, and collaborating with their dev team.

## Offer 3 — Product Strategy *(Track B / "not sure yet")*

- **Who it's for:** Founders who need direction *before* committing to a build — what to build, for whom, and why.
- **What you get:** Goal & user definition → opportunity and flow mapping → prioritized product direction → a clear, actionable plan (and the design starting point). Reframes a tangled idea into an obvious path.
- **Timeline:** ~1–2 weeks, or a focused workshop.
- **Starting price:** *Workshop from $X,XXX; engagement from $X,XXX.* — confirm.
- **Positioning note:** The entry point for "not sure yet" quiz answers. Sells Sam's #1 strength (strategy) and naturally leads to Offer 2.
